Royal Enfield

1.The GT 650 showcases Royal Enfield's legendary parallel twin-cylinder engine, backed with an air/oil-cooled parallel twin configuration. The 648cc engine offers 47 horsepower at 7150 RPM and a maximum of 52 Nm of torque at 5250 RPM.

2.The Royal Enfield Continental GT 650 would be a good choice if you are looking for a bike that offers outright thrills, meaty mid-range, stylish looks, and a powerful cruising machine. The GT's sporty riding position makes it much more suitable for twisty mountain roads and even high speed straight line blasts.

3.It is the first modern twin cylinder motorcycle developed by the company. In 2017 it was revealed, inspired by the 1960s Interceptor which had a 750cc engine. The Interceptor 650 was unveiled alongside the Continental GT 650, which shares the same engine.
